Predictability turns out to be more important than maximizing utilization. To provide better predictability to stakeholders and customers, start by focusing on stabilizing the rate that work items flow through the system.

Lead Time is the average time a work items spends in a system Operational definition: From when a team commits to work until PR merge

Work in Progress (WIP) is the set of work started but not yet completed or abandoned Operational definition: Use the same start and end time criteria as used for Lead Time

Throughput is the average rate the work items enter and exit the system Operational definition: Calculated as Lead Time divided by WIP
